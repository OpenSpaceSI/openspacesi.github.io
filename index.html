Innovation for the Southern Illinois Community

We aim to cultivate an entrepreneurial mindset in Southern Illinois through a collaborative, engaging, and exciting environment centered around a "DIY" culture. We are partnering with local school districts and universities to foster technical skills across all age groups.

Services

Everything you need to make your digital dreams come true


LEARN

We have everything from 3D printers to robotics kits. No matter what your experience level, there are gadgets and toolkits here for you to learn on.

BUILD

The focus of OpenSpace is creation. Feel free to use our tools to build your next big app, the solution to a problem that has been bothering you, a quick fix for the garage door opener back home, or anything else your heart desires. We have a 22 computer computer lab setup for development, and the tools at your disposale to create whatever your heart desires.

CONTRIBUTE

The most important part of OpenSpace is belonging to a community. That community is open to empowering you in any way we can, and we hope you return the favor! We have opportunities for you to help others 1 on 1, teach courses, and even plan events. If you want a venue for sharing your knowledge and passion with the Southern Illinois Community, this is the place.
